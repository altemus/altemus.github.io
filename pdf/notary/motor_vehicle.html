Forms, Publications, and Maps

PennDOT is committed to providing its customers with services in an eco-friendly and accessible manner. To that end, most of our forms, publications and maps are available in a digital format by searching the database below.

Print copies of select documents may ordered using our Sales Store List, which also includes free items.

If you are seeking a map not in this list, please check our Maps page.

For information on driver licensing and motor vehicle documents, please contact Driver and Vehicle Services. For information on any other type of PennDOT document, please email Office Services staff.

When viewing PDF documents on this site, it is recommended to use the latest version of Internet Explorer.
